Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Clear Site West Island is fully licensed and insured, ensuring professional and reliable service for all our customers.

What are your rates for junk removal services?

Our rates depend on the volume and type of junk to be removed. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees and can offer a free estimate upon request.

What types of junk and refuse do you accept?

We accept a wide range of local construction junk and residential household refuse, including debris from renovations, old furniture, appliances, yard waste, and general household trash.

What materials are strictly prohibited?

For the safety of our crew and community compliance, we maintain a zero-tolerance transportation ban on hazardous household waste (HHW). This includes loose asbestos fibers, wet chemical lead paints, storage propane tanks, industrial solvents, or medical pollutants.

What are the requirements and discharges for each service tier?

Each tier has distinct operational parameters: Unprepared Pick-Up requires full site access for our crew; Curbside Collection requires all junk positioned at the property line prior to pickup; On-Site Disposal Meeting requires the client to meet at the eco-centre gate with resident ID for fee off-set; and Full Co-Pilot is passive for vehicle operation (a licensed ClearSite professional operates the vehicle at all times due to insurance compliance).
