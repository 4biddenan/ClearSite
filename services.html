Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common resident questions regarding load criteria, stair charges, and bad weather operations.

Most West Island municipalities provide free annual disposal credits to residents. By choosing our Disposal Meeting or Co-Pilot services, we meet you at the local eco-center so you can use these free credits to offset tip fees entirely.
Ground level or driveway loading has $0 travel surcharge. Hand-carrying items down indoor stairs (per flight of 10+ steps) adds a standard labor flight fee. Disposal Meeting and Full Co-Pilot tiers do not charge stairs fees if loaded to ground level first.
Yes, we operate year-round. However, if extreme weather makes transport or loading unsafe, dispatch will contact you to reschedule or shift to commercial routing.
Never. We believe in 100% upfront transparency. Our quoted estimates include all travel distance fuel, loading labor, sweep-up, and municipal sorting. Dump disposal fees are fully absorbed in Unprepared Pick-Up, or offset using your residency allowance in On-Site Disposal Meeting & Full Co-Pilot.
Yes! We specialize in post-renovation masonry hauling. However, dense aggregates are subject to truck axle weight safety regulations. Any heavy load exceeding 3 m³ is automatically restructured into multiple safe trips to protect your driveway and our vehicles.
For the safety of our crew and community compliance, we maintain a zero-tolerance transportation ban on hazardous household waste (HHW). This includes loose asbestos fibers, wet chemical lead paints, storage propane tanks, industrial solvents, or medical pollutants.
Each tier has distinct operational parameters: Unprepared Pick-Up requires full site access for our crew; Curbside Collection requires all junk positioned at the property line prior to pickup; On-Site Disposal Meeting requires the client to meet at the eco-centre gate with resident ID for fee off-set; and Full Co-Pilot is passive for vehicle operation (a licensed ClearSite professional operates the vehicle at all times due to insurance compliance).
